About

Veteran teacher seeking to pursue new challenges.

Me

I like cats and coffee, and I'm not afraid to take down an entire dark chocolate bar in one sitting. I have built a career around teaching, both math and computer science, and have also spent time as a mortgage guy. Seattle was my home for many years, graduating from University of Washington with a degrees in EE and Math. I also spent time in Baltimore, earning a Masters at Johns Hopkins. I now live in north Texas with my family, getting out to find nature outside of Texas whenever possible. I love camping, watching football, and puzzles (and coding).

My next career goal is to become a successful and productive software developer. There is something about building a project that is uber-satisfying like nothing else can be. My logical mind is well-suited to problem solving and I enjoy a good challenge. I particularly love when a student brings me a new deep-rooted error in a new situation that requires a thorough round of debugging to gut out the problem. As a teacher of computer science, I have spent years honing the skills and mindset to thrive in the software industry. If you or anyone you know is looking for a promising candidate, please don't hesitate to reach out.
